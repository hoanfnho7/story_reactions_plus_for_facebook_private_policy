Crystal Clear Promise

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information.

This includes, but is not limited to:

Single Purpose Design

The Extension uses your browser's localStorage for one purpose only: to save your customized list of favorite emojis.

Minimal & Essential Only

We follow the principle of least privilege - requesting only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to enhance your Facebook story experience. No unnecessary access is requested or granted.
